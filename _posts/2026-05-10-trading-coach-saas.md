## What I Built

Trading Coach SaaS is a product concept for a beginner-friendly AI trading coach — the kind of tool I wish existed when I first started learning markets. The idea is a guided, opinionated paper trading experience where a coach walks you through setups, explains what to look for, and gives feedback on your trades.

This is a separate project from Apex (my personal trading terminal) — Trading Coach is designed as a potential SaaS product for people who are brand new to trading and need guided structure, not a raw workspace.

## The Problem

Most trading education is either: watch 100 hours of YouTube videos, or pay $500/month for a Discord group. Neither is particularly structured or personalized. A beginner needs real-time, contextual feedback — not pre-recorded lectures.

## Concept Features

- **Onboarding Flow** — collects trading goals, risk tolerance, available time, and learning style
- **Guided Setups** — the coach surfaces specific chart setups and walks through the reasoning in plain English
- **Paper Trade Tracker** — log simulated trades with coach commentary on the decision
- **Learning Modules** — bite-sized lessons on concepts like support/resistance, volume, and risk management
- **Progress Dashboard** — tracks win rate, consistency, and completion of learning milestones

## Tech Stack

- React 18 + TypeScript
- Vite (port 5176)
- Tailwind CSS
- Planned: Anthropic Claude API for coaching responses

## Status

This is an early-stage concept — the UI scaffolding and core flows are built out. The next step is integrating a real AI coaching layer and deciding on a business model (subscription vs. one-time).

## Why I'm Building This

After spending time on my own paper trading journey, I realized the hardest part isn't access to tools — it's knowing what to look for and building consistent habits. A guided coach that gives specific, actionable feedback could genuinely help beginners skip years of expensive trial and error.
