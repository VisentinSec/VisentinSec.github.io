_Home Pro — clean, professional website for a family-owned remodeling company_

## What I Built

Home Pro is a production-ready business website for a family-owned remodeling and home improvement company serving Mississippi and Alabama. It's a modern, fast React site designed to generate leads, showcase past work, and make it easy for homeowners to reach out.

## The Brief

Family business, existing customers, no online presence. Needed a site that looks professional on mobile, loads fast, and makes it dead simple for someone to request a quote. Nothing fancy — just clean, trustworthy, and functional.

## Features

- **Services Pages** — detailed pages for each service offered (kitchens, bathrooms, roofing, additions, etc.)
- **Portfolio Gallery** — before/after project photos
- **Quote Request Form** — simple contact form that captures job type, location, and description
- **Service Area Map** — shows covered counties across Mississippi and Alabama
- **Testimonials Section** — social proof from past clients
- **Mobile-First Design** — built for homeowners searching on their phones

## Tech Stack

- React 18 + TypeScript
- Vite
- Tailwind CSS
- Fully static — no backend required

## Design Approach

I kept the design clean and trustworthy — neutral tones, strong typography, real project photos. The kind of site that makes a homeowner feel confident picking up the phone. No flashy animations, no distracting elements. Just the information they need to make a decision.

## What I Learned

Working on a client site with real constraints taught me a lot about scoping. The client doesn't care about tech stack — they care about whether it shows up on Google and whether customers can find the phone number. That focus on actual user goals over technical choices is something I carry into every project now.
